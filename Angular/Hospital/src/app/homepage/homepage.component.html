 WELCOME TO MEDICO MEDICAL CENTRE AND HOSPITAL


Medico medical centre and hospital is a multidisciplinary Nursing Home with modern facilities, equipments, and health care personnel, catering to the health care needs of the people of this District since 1993. New infrastructure facilities, diagnostic tools and specialties were opened in successive years.

As the modern Laparoscopic surgical facilities are included for the first time in this southern part of the district in the year 2000, this hospital has earned a name as a specialty centre for laparoscopic Surgery & Infertility Care. However, the other specialties are also functioning in this hospital including General Medicine, Cardiology, Neurology, Paediatrics, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Orthopaedics, ENT, Urology and Anesthesiology.
